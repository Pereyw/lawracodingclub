WELCOME MESSAGE

Lawra Coding Club (LCC)
Learning is a collaborative effort which needs to be taking serious in every developing community.
The advancement in technology has a lot of impact on the lives of people in every society as we can see a lot of people or communities flourishing in the field of technology.
We the people can also be like them or even more than them:
this can be achieved if we all come together and learn how to developers of our own product rather than being end user at all the time. Mission Statement

It is our hope and wish that every child or the youth will be able to know the essence of writing codes (syntax) to command the computer to perform a task because the computer is a tool that takes instructions from the user.

Vision Statement

Digital Literacy is what we are in for, with this initiative we of the view that computer literacy is an important asset in the municipality as almost 85% of the citizens within the municipality are using smartphones. Let’s support the vision and dream of digitalization as we train ourselves towards programming.
